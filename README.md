America250 is a game based on the American Revolutionary War. The game takes place in July 1781 during the Battle of Green Spring and the Siege of Yorktown.

## Battle of Green Spring
The Battle of Green Spring, fought on July 6, 1781, was the largest open-field battle of the Revolutionary War in Virginia. British general
Charles Cornwallis set an ambush for 23-year-old general Marquis de Lafayette and his 4,000 American soldiers. Lafayette believed he had an
opportunity to strike the British rearguard. However, Cornwallis had laid a trap with 7,000 concealed British soldiers in the woods. Lafayette
sent his advance guard, commanded by Anthony Wayne, to engage the British pickets. Heavily outnumbered, the Americans faced total annihilation.
To save his soldiers, Wayne ordered a direct bayonent charge into the main British line. This aggressive attack stunned Cornwallis, who hesitated,
believing that he was facing the main American army rather than just a small advance guard. The pause gave Wayne time to retreat, and 
Lafayette's army survived intact.

## Siege of Yorktown
The Siege of Yorktown was the final major land engagement of the Revolutionary War. Allied American and French forces led by George Washington
trapped General Cornwallis's army in Yorktown, Virginia, forcing him to surrender. Cornwallis had moved his army of 8,000 British troops to 
Yorktown, expecting to be resupplied by the British navy. Recognizing an opportunity, Washington moved 17,000 American troops to Virginia and 
a French fleet secured Chesapeake Bay, blocking a British escape or resupplial.
